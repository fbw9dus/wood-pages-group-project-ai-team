Sotwood is usually woods from gymnosperm trees such as pines and spruces. Softwoods
are not necessarily softer than hardwods.[1] In both groups there is an enormous
variation in actual wood hardness, the range of density in hardwoods completely
including that of softwoods. Some hardwoods (e.g. balsa) are softer than most
softwoods, while the hardest hardwoods are much harder than any sofwood. The woods
of longleaf pine, Douglas fir, and yew are much harder in the mechanical sense than
several hardwoods.
